Day 1: Passing

Why is passing so important? Who are passers and what are their different roles?

  • When the opposing team is serving, passing is the first touch that your team gets. Without a good first pass, it is harder for the setter to make a good set and harder for the hitter to get a kill. If you shank the ball, then your team immediately loses the point. Serve receive is always stressed as the most important part of the game.
  • The main passers are the Libero and Outside hitters.
